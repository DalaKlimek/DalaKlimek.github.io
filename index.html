About

I am a digital artist and photographer from Bavaria with a great interest in Games and Movies. Being inspired by my artistic friends, I started drawing in 2011 and since then am working on becoming better at my craft.

Art has always been a way for me to put my thoughts into reality, where its visible and may inspire people!
I fell in love with creating complex worlds through characterdesigns, landscapes and Illustrations, but am also interested in hearing others ideas, so that I am now working as a freelancer since 2015, while still going to High School.



For my drawings I use a wacoom tablet and Paint tool sai but also like to experiment with traditional art where I use pen and paper,charcoal,chalk,ink and watercolors.
Feel free to check out my portfolio and comment on it if you want!
